About Me

Hey! My name is Salaar Khan, and I am so glad you chose our podcast. I am currently an IT student at George Mason University, and some of my hobbies include Football, Filmmaking, Screenwriting, Gaming, and I also love to mess around on Garageband and make some music! Check out the SoundCloud widget below! I started this podcast with my good friend Cameron back in 2020. For years we planned to create a podcast, a medium where we are able to share our love for football, and to be able to talk about it. I have been a devout Denver Broncos fan since the beginning of the 2013 NFL season. Yes, you can call me a bandwagon, but my love for football started because of one significant game, and that game was week 1 of the 2013 NFL season, where Peyton Manning and the Broncos defeated the Ravens 49-27. No, I did not become a fan of this team because they made it to the Super Bowl! Anyways, that game is what sparked my love for football and this team, which I will represent for the rest of my life. Go Broncos! I chose not to add anything about Cameron due to privacy reasons.
